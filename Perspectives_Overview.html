For Your Consideration

Before reviewing these pages and their contents, please be aware of two time and space-saving decisions I made when creating them:

  • I do not detail my past work in this section like is often done in web portfolio sites like this one. I decided this because each quantitative and statistical project’s success is dependent on its own unique, dynamic properties. Thus, at best, past work can only serve as an exemplar of one’s statistical and quantitative work process. Do note that I present an example report I created on this section’s last page, “#3,” and in this website’s next section, “Gallery,” I present other images of my past work. 
  • I do not describe my perspectives and approaches to all relevant quantitative and statistical work aspects; instead, only a select few. Furthermore, the ones I do, are tied directly to data handling. So, equally important parts of the quantitative work process, like planning and designing the work/research charter, are not discussed. If you want more information about my perspectives and approaches to areas of work not highlighted in this section, don’t hesitate to get in touch with me and ask!
